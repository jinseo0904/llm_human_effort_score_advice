Research Study Information

You are being invited to participate in a research study. Please read this form carefully and ask any questions you may have before agreeing to participate.

Purpose of the Study:

The purpose of this study is to understand how people write advice responses with the assistance of AI tools.

What You Will Be Asked To Do:

You will be asked to write an advice response to a given situation. You may use AI assistance tools if you wish. We will track your interactions and effort metrics during this process.

Risks and Benefits:

There are minimal risks associated with this study. The benefits include contributing to research on human-AI collaboration.

Confidentiality:

Your responses will be kept confidential. Your User ID will be used to link your responses but will not be shared publicly.

Voluntary Participation:

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

Questions:

If you have any questions about this study, please contact the research team.
